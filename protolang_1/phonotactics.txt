N=m,n
Stress is on the second-to-last syllable unless specified.
Diphthongs and stress:
    If the word has only 1 syllable AND has a diphthong, the stress is on the first vowel of the diphthong.
    if the stressed vowel is a diphthong elsewhere, the stress is on the second vowel of the diphthong.
